NewText: Text Reinvented for Friends

     It's Real Take A Lot's of Time to Develop Pattern from Text for 'Good Morning' , 'Thank You' and 'I am Happy'.

    NewText Is As Simple As It's Name. You Just Need To Type Any Text In Text Box And Than Press The Pattern Icon or Emoji, On Which You Wants Reinvent Text.

    You Can Create Pattern and Reinvent Text For Any Text , Like 'Your Name' , 'Your Friend Name' , 'Good Morning' and Anything Else That You and Your Friends Love to See and Share.

    You Can Reinvent Text With Following Patterns In NewText :

    - #######

    - %%%%%%%

    - ooooooo

    - ~~~~~~~

    - &&&&&&&

    - ()()()()()()()

    - +++++++

    - =======

    - ||||||||||||||

    - {}{}{}{}{}{}{}

    - [][][][][][][]

    - <<<<<<<

    - >>>>>>>

    - )()()()()()()(

    - }{}{}{}{}{}{}{

    - ][][][][][][][
